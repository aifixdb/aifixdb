Privacy Policy

What we collect

When you register for an API key, we collect:

  • Email address — to verify your account and communicate about the service
  • Display name (optional) — shown alongside your submitted fixes

When you submit fixes, we store the technical content you provide (problem title, error messages, solutions, stack tags). This content is public by design.

Why we collect it

  • Email: account verification, API key delivery, abuse prevention
  • Display name: attribution on submitted fixes

We do not use your email for marketing, newsletters, or any purpose other than account management.

How we store it

  • Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database on servers in Helsinki, Finland (Hetzner)
  • API keys are hashed (SHA-256) — we cannot see your key after generation
  • Traffic is encrypted via HTTPS (Cloudflare)

Who has access

Only the service administrator. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — know what data we have about you
  • Rectification — correct your data
  • Erasure — delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability — export your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Delete your account

Send an email to privacy@nocodework.io with the subject "Delete aifixdb account" and the email address you registered with. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Cookies

We use localStorage (not cookies) to save your API key for voting. No tracking cookies, no analytics.

Data controller

NoCodeWork (nocodework.io)
